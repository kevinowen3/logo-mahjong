How to Play

Logo is a solitaire matching game. Your goal is to clear the whole board by removing every tile.

Basic move

Click two tiles that show the same logo to remove them as a pair. Keep matching pairs until the board is empty — you win!

Which tiles can you pick?

A tile is “free” (selectable) only if:

  1. Nothing is stacked on top of it, and
  2. At least one side (left or right) is open.

Tiles sandwiched between two neighbours are locked until you clear what’s blocking them. Click a selected tile again, or click the empty board, to deselect.

Toolbar helpers

  • Hint — highlights a playable matching pair.
  • Undo — takes back your last match.
  • Shuffle — rearranges the remaining tiles when you’re stuck.
  • ▶ New Game — the small triangle picks a difficulty; the main button starts a new game at your current level.
  • Logos — browse the company marks in your current level.

Difficulty levels

  • Newb — 44 tiles, gentle introduction.
  • Padawan — 72 tiles, bigger board, more layers.
  • Smarty Pants — 144 tiles, the classic turtle.

Tips

  • Work from the top down — high-layer tiles unlock everything below.
  • Edge tiles free up faster than buried middle ones.
  • Don’t always grab the first match: if a logo has 4 tiles on the board and 2 are free, sometimes you’d rather pair one with a different copy later.
  • Stuck? Use Hint or Shuffle.

About Logo

Logo — a solitaire game patterned after the Chinese tile game called Mahjong.
